- Projects
Throughout the June of Summer 2020, Zunair worked on 4 different projects for the course 'Interactive Media'. Have a look at one of his first works involving media on the web!
- Productive Quarantine (30MFF)
This was the first assignment that required students in groups to make a film in only 30 minutes. The film was required to be posted on the web.
- Comic Strip
This was the second assignment that involved an interactive comic with a story that requires the user to 'choose their own adventure'
- CUYAGUA (Sound Project)
This was the third assignment that involved a sound map that takes you on an auditory journery to CUYAGUA
- VENGEANCE (Film Project)
This was the fourth assignment that involved a film with a story about vengeance.
